Regex form field validations

Username must contain 6 - 12 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g. test@domain.com

Password must be alphanumeric (@, _ and - are also allowed) and be 8 - 20 characters long

Phone must be a valid telephone number (11 digits) long

Slug must contain lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens and be 8 - 20 characters long
